Boost your Inspiration

As we experienced ourselves that it is can be very hard to write unusual and surprising greeting cards.
We decided to combine the classic approach of writing with text-generating neuronal networks.
Our algorithm decides - based on AI - what words fit best for your occasion and mood.
Additionally it generates phrases in the style of different people, your personal Ghostwriters.
Et voilà: a innovative greeting text !


Hybrid Approach

Behind the scenes we use a combination of predefined structure and free text generation.
We use the Machine Learning Model FitBERT to smartly customize sentences and with the
help of Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN's) we provide completely new sentence creations



Our Team





This project was realised as part of the course "Komputer und Creativität" of the Technical University Munich.
One aim of this course is to understand and develop computational creativity such as this software.
